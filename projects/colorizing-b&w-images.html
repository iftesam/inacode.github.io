Colorizing Black-and-White Images

Algorithm:

With the neural network, Black-and-White colorization was possible.
The algorithm consists of four steps, which are:

1. Converting black and white to Lab photo
2. Try to understand the contents of the photo
3. Specific Database comparison between black-and-white grade scale pixels
4. Try to map colors from the database
